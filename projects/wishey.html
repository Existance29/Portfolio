Wishey

Wishey is a budgeting app that allows young adults to keep track of their monthly budget, income, expenses and savings.

Project Overview

In 2020, I joined the Swift Accelerator Programme, an 8 month programme where I learn to develop IOS apps in swift. For the final project, I worked in a team to develop an IOS app. We developed Wishey, a budgeting app. My primary role was as a designer, where I was responsible for creating its UI and UX.

Wishey's goal is to help adults and students keep track of their spendings, plan their monthly budget and help them make responsible financial choices. It provides insights on users' spendings through pie charts, bar graphs and lists.
It also has a wishlist to keep track of gifts and items that users would like to purchase. Additionally, it also allows users to plan their expenses for various categories income and projected savings in mind.
