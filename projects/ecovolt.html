EcoVolt

A platform for analysing energy consumption and carbon emissions for telecom companies and provides personalised insights/recommendations to reduce their carbon footprint

Project Overview

A website developed as a group project for the full stack development module coursework. Its main goal is to help local telecomm companies improve sustainability practices and lower operational costs by reducing their energy consumption and carbon footprint.

Tech Stack:
- HTML
- CSS
- Javascript
- NodeJS
- Express
- SQL

This platform has a dashboard that tracks various sustainability metrics from a company's data centers and cell towers. It also generates monthly report with GenAI-driven analysis and tailored recommendations on how to reduce carbon footprints.
Users can share about their company's sustainability efforts through an activity feed. In addition, they can enroll in sustainability-focused courses and participate in events to earn points which can be exchanged for rewards.
